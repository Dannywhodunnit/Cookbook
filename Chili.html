Chili

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of kidney beans (15oz)
  • 2 cans of diced tomatoes (14.5oz)
  • 1 can of tomato sauce (15oz)
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Mexene Chili Powder
  • 1 cup of diced yellow onion
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 8 oz of spaghetti noodles
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon powder
  • 1 tbsp Grippos seasoning
  • 1-2 tsp paprika
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Brown beef in skillet and strain, add salt and pepper to your liking
  2. Add minced garlic and onions, cook until fragrant
  3. Remove skillet from heat and transfer contents into a large pot
  4. With the pot on the burner on medium and contents inside, add tomato sauce
  5. Add in the beans and tomatoes without straining
  6. After 5 minutes of cooking stir in the chili powder, cinnamon powder, grippos seasoning, and paprika
  7. Occasionally stir, remove from heat after 15 minutes
  8. Optional: Serve with shredded cheese, crackers, sour cream and/or peanut butter sandwich
  9. Enjoy!

Chili has a unique place in our cultures and history, with many variations there is not ONE correct way to make it. For more on the dish, read this article on AllRecipes
