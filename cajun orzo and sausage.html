Cajun Orzo and Sausage

Not to be confused with Shawarma Chicken and Orzo Skillet, this is a spicier and creamier dish

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 12 oz. andouille sausage, sliced
  • 8 oz. orzo
  • 1 (14-oz.) can fire roasted diced tomatoes
  • 2 c. low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 tsp. cajun seasoning
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 oz. cream cheese, cut into cubes
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. In a large deep skillet over medium heat, add the olive oil
  2. Add garlic and cook until fragrant
  3. Add sausage and cook until warmed through and golden, about 2 minutes
  4. Add orzo and stir for 1 minute
  5. Add diced tomatoes, broth, and cajun seasoning, add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until orzo is cooked through and tender, about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally
  7. Add cream cheese and stir until melted, then stir in green onions
  8. Enjoy!
