Shawarma Chicken and Orzo Skillet

Unlike the Cajun Orzo and Sausage, this skillet features fresh mediterranean flavors!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb. chicken tenders or chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1 tbsp. ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp. smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 5 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 c. low-sodium chicken broth
  • 12 oz. orzo (about 2 c.)
  • 1/4 c. finely chopped fresh dill, plus more for serving
  • 1/2 English cucumber, halved and thinly sliced
  • 2 c. cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 c. crumbled feta (about 2 oz.)
  • 1 tbsp. fresh lemon juice

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, toss chicken, coriander, paprika, red pepper flakes, cinnamon, 3/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  2. In a large high-sided skillet over medium heat, heat 2 tablespoons oil. Add half of chicken mixture and cook, turning halfway through, until golden and cooked through, 2-3 minutes per side. Transfer chicken to a plate. Repeat with 1 tbsp more oil and remaining chicken mixture
  3. Wipe any dark bits from pan with a clean paper towel, if necessary. Return skillet to medium heat
  4. Cook garlic and 1 tablespoon oil, stirring, until garlic is fragrant
  5. Stir in broth, orzo, and 1/2 teaspoon salt and bring to a boil
  6. Reduce to heat to medium-low and gently simmer, stirring frequently, until orzo is tender, 12-14 mins
  7. Remove skillet from heat, stir in dill, and top with chicken mixture
  8. In a medium bowl, toss cucumber, tomatoes, feta, lemon juice, remaining 1 tablespoon oil, and a pinch of salt
  9. Spoon cucumber salad over chicken mixture
  10. top with more dill
  11. Enjoy!
