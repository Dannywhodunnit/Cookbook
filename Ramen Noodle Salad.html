Ramen Noodle Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 bags of brocoli cole slaw
  • 2 packs of chicken ramen
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons Rice Vinegar
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup green onions, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the ramen flavor packet, apple cider vinegar, olive oil, rice vinegar, and sugar until mixed well
  2. Fill a large bowl with the brocoli cole slaw
  3. Crush the Ramen noodles and mix them in with the slaw
  4. Pour the wet mix over the slaw and rotate the mix with your hands until it's evenly coated
  5. Refrigerate for 1 hour
  6. Before serving add the sliced almonds and green onions, either mixed in or topped
  7. Enjoy!
